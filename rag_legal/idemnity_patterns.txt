INDEMNIFICATION RISK PATTERNS

BEST PRACTICE:
- Indemnity limited to breaches caused by indemnifying party.
- Includes defense control mechanism.
- Includes reasonable attorney fees.
- Often includes liability cap.

RISK CONDITIONS:
- Unlimited indemnity.
- Indemnity applies regardless of fault.
- No cap on damages.
- No exclusion of consequential damages.
- No reciprocal indemnity in mutual NDA.

ESCALATION RULE:
If indemnity is unlimited AND no liability cap exists,
Severity should be HIGH or CRITICAL.
