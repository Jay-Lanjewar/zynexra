LIMITATION OF LIABILITY INTELLIGENCE

BEST PRACTICE:
- Cap tied to contract value.
- Exclusion of indirect or consequential damages.
- Carve-outs for fraud or willful misconduct.

RISK CONDITIONS:
- No limitation clause at all.
- Unlimited liability exposure.
- Indemnity without liability cap.
- Cap applied only to one party.

ESCALATION RULE:
Absence of limitation clause combined with indemnity clause increases litigation exposure significantly.
