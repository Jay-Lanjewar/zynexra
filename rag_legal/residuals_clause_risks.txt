RESIDUALS CLAUSE RISK INTELLIGENCE

Residuals clauses allow use of information retained in memory.

HIGH RISK CONDITIONS:
- No restriction on use.
- No exclusion of trade secrets.
- No time limitation.
- No obligation to avoid deliberate memorization.

COMMERCIAL IMPACT:
Residuals clause may effectively nullify confidentiality protection.

ESCALATION RULE:
If residuals clause allows unrestricted use,
Severity should be HIGH or CRITICAL.
