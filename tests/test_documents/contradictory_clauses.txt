SERVICE AGREEMENT (EXCERPT)

Section 5.1 - Term. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of five (5) years. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any reason or no reason, upon thirty (30) days' written notice.

Section 5.2 - Non-Compete. During the Term and for a period of three (3) years after termination, Service Provider shall not engage in any business that competes with Client anywhere in the world.

Section 5.3 - Non-Compete Termination. The non-compete obligation in Section 5.2 shall immediately and automatically terminate upon any termination of this Agreement for any reason. Neither party shall be bound by any post-termination restrictions.

Section 8.1 - Confidentiality. Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement and continue for a period of five (5) years after termination.

Section 8.2 - Confidentiality Termination. All confidentiality obligations shall immediately cease upon termination of this Agreement. No confidentiality obligations shall survive.

Section 12.1 - Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

Section 12.2 - Governing Law (Alternative). This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the United Kingdom, and the parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts.

Section 15.1 - Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

Section 15.2 - Entire Agreement (Superseding). This Agreement supersedes all prior agreements, including the Service Agreement dated January 1, 2026, which remains in full force and effect.
