EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] by and between [Company], a [Jurisdiction] corporation ("Company"), and [Employee] ("Employee").

1. AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT. Employee's employment with Company is at-will. Either party may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or advance notice.

2. POSITION AND DUTIES. Employee shall serve as [Position] and perform such duties as may be assigned by Company. Employee shall devote their full business time and attention to Company's business.

3. COMPENSATION. Company shall pay Employee an annual base salary of [Amount], payable in accordance with Company's standard payroll practices.

4. BENEFITS. Employee shall be eligible to participate in Company's standard benefit plans, including health insurance, retirement, and paid time off, as may be in effect from time to time.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY. During and after employment, Employee shall hold in confidence and not disclose or use any confidential or proprietary information of Company, including without limitation trade secrets, business plans, customer lists, financial data, and technical information.

6. INVENTION ASSIGNMENT. Employee agrees to assign to Company all inventions, discoveries, and works of authorship that Employee creates during employment that: (a) relate to Company's actual or anticipated business; (b) result from or are suggested by work performed for Company; or (c) are developed using Company's equipment, supplies, or confidential information. This assignment obligation does not apply to inventions that Employee develops entirely on Employee's own time without using Company's equipment, supplies, facilities, or trade secret information, and that do not relate to Company's business or actual or demonstrably anticipated research or development.

7. NON-COMPETITION. For a period of six (6) months following termination of employment, Employee shall not, within a fifty (50) mile radius of Company's principal office, engage in any business that is directly competitive with Company's business as conducted during Employee's employment.

8. NON-SOLICITATION. For a period of twelve (12) months following termination of employment, Employee shall not solicit or induce any employee of Company to terminate their employment.

9. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
