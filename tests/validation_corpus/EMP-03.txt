EXECUTIVE EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Executive Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] by and between [Company], a [Jurisdiction] corporation ("Company"), and [Executive] ("Executive").

1. TERM. The initial term of employment shall be three (3) years, automatically renewing for successive one-year periods unless either party provides sixty (60) days' written notice of non-renewal prior to expiration.

2. POSITION. Executive shall serve as [Title] and report to the Board of Directors.

3. COMPENSATION.
   (a) Base Salary: [Amount] per year.
   (b) Annual Bonus: Target of [Percentage] of base salary based on performance.
   (c) Equity: [Number] restricted stock units vesting over four (4) years.

4. BENEFITS. Executive shall participate in all benefit plans generally available to Company's executives.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY. Executive shall not disclose or use any confidential or proprietary information of Company during or after employment.

6. INVENTION ASSIGNMENT. Executive agrees to assign to Company all inventions and intellectual property created during employment that relate to Company's business or are developed using Company's resources. This obligation excludes inventions that Executive develops entirely on Executive's own time and that do not relate to Company's business.

7. CHANGE OF CONTROL. Upon a Change of Control (as defined herein), all unvested equity awards shall immediately accelerate and become fully vested. If Executive's employment is terminated within twelve (12) months following a Change of Control, Executive shall receive: (i) severance equal to twenty-four (24) months of base salary; (ii) continued health benefits for twenty-four (24) months; and (iii) any unpaid bonus for the year of termination.

8. NON-COMPETITION. For twelve (12) months following termination, Executive shall not engage in any business competitive with Company within a one hundred (100) mile radius of Company's headquarters.

9. NON-SOLICITATION. For twelve (12) months following termination, Executive shall not solicit any Company employee or customer.

10. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
