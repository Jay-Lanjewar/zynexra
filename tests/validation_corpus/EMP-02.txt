EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] by and between [Company] ("Company") and [Employee] ("Employee").

1. AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT. Employee's employment with Company is at-will.

2. POSITION AND DUTIES. Employee shall serve as [Position] and perform duties as assigned.

3. COMPENSATION. Base salary of [Amount] per year.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY. Employee shall hold in confidence all confidential and proprietary information of Company during and after employment.

5. INVENTION ASSIGNMENT. Employee hereby assigns and agrees to assign to Company all inventions, discoveries, improvements, designs, works of authorship, and other intellectual property, whether or not patentable or copyrightable, that Employee may conceive, develop, create, or reduce to practice, alone or jointly with others, during the period of Employee's employment with Company, whether during or outside of working hours, whether on or off Company's premises, and whether or not at Company's request. Employee acknowledges that all such works shall be the sole and exclusive property of Company. This assignment obligation extends to all inventions and intellectual property conceived, developed, or created at any time during employment, anywhere in the world, using any resources, and regardless of whether they relate to Company's business.

6. NON-COMPETITION. For a period of eighteen (18) months following the termination of Employee's employment for any reason, Employee shall not, anywhere in the world, directly or indirectly, own, manage, operate, control, be employed by, participate in, or be connected in any manner with any business that competes with Company's business.

7. NON-SOLICITATION. For a period of eighteen (18) months following termination, Employee shall not solicit any employee, customer, or vendor of Company.

8. SEVERABILITY. If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

9. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Georgia.
