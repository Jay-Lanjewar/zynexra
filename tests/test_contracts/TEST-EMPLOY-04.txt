EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of May 1, 2025, between InnovateTech Solutions Inc., a Washington corporation ("Company"), and Sarah Chen ("Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee shall serve as Chief Technology Officer, reporting to the Chief Executive Officer. Employee shall devote Employee's full time and best efforts to the performance of Employee's duties.

2. COMPENSATION
Base salary: $225,000 per year, payable in accordance with Company's standard payroll schedule.
Signing bonus: $50,000, payable within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date.
Annual bonus: Target of 40% of base salary, based on individual and company performance.

3. EQUITY
Subject to Board approval, Employee shall receive a grant of 100,000 stock options vesting over four (4) years with a one (1) year cliff.

4. BENEFITS
Employee shall be eligible to participate in all employee benefit programs that Company establishes and makes available to its employees, including health insurance, 401(k), and paid time off.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee agrees to hold in confidence all proprietary and confidential information of the Company. This obligation survives termination of employment.

6. INVENTION ASSIGNMENT
Employee agrees to assign to the Company all inventions, discoveries, and improvements made during employment that relate to the Company's business.

7. NON-COMPETE
For a period of eighteen (18) months following termination of employment, Employee shall not engage in any business that competes with the Company's business within the State of Washington.

8. NON-SOLICITATION
For a period of twelve (12) months following termination, Employee shall not solicit any employee or customer of the Company.

9. TERMINATION
Employment is at-will and may be terminated by either party at any time with or without cause. Upon termination without cause, Employee shall receive severance of six (6) months' base salary.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Washington.
