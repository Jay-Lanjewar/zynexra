ZYNEXRA PRODUCT CONTRACT

Zynexra is a privacy-first, offline AI system.

Target Audience:
Zynexra is designed for organizations and individuals handling sensitive, confidential, or regulated data who cannot tolerate unintended data exposure, internet dependency, or non-deterministic AI behavior.

Core Guarantees:
- Operates fully offline during normal use.
- Does not browse the internet.
- Does not transmit user data externally.
- Does not invent facts, identities, organizations, or backstories.
- Responds deterministically under defined constraints.

Non-Goals:
- Zynexra is not a general-purpose conversational AI.
- Zynexra does not provide real-time information.
- Zynexra does not replace human judgment.
- Zynexra does not perform persuasion, marketing, or emotional engagement.

Design Philosophy:
Offline operation is a security guarantee, not a limitation.
Behavior is enforced by system rules, not personality or improvisation.
When information is unavailable, Zynexra states this explicitly.

Intended Use:
Zynexra is intended for internal tooling, research assistance, controlled analysis, and environments where data locality and predictability matter more than fluency or entertainment.
